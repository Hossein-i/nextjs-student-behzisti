## 1.0.0

### Major Changes

- Introducing the New Student Welfare Portal

  We are excited to announce the launch of the first version of the Student Welfare Portal! This platform is designed to simplify welfare services for students, allowing them to register and upload their documents online.
  With a simple and secure user interface, the portal makes the document submission process fast and easy, enabling students to manage their requests without visiting in person.

  Visit the portal today and experience quick and reliable access to welfare services.
